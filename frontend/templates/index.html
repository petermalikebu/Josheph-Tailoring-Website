About Us

Joseph is a highly skilled tailor from the heart of Malawi, residing in the picturesque Zomba District in Songani area, within the peaceful village of Katete. With over 15 years of hands-on experience, Joseph has honed his craft, becoming a respected name in the tailoring community. His journey began as a young apprentice, learning the art of tailoring under the guidance of seasoned craftsmen in his village.
